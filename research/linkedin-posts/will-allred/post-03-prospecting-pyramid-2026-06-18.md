Sellers, this pyramid is your market. Most prospecting messaging is optimized for the green. If you'd stop talking about yourself, you'd 3x your opportunity zone.

***

But Will, I get a 5% reply rate with a simple pitch of our software.

Great... sounds like you have good product marketing and a well targeted list for that message. This advice will still help.

***

The biggest mental flip in this pyramid is that it's the buyer's perspective.

I'm buying now.
I'm open to it.
I'm not thinking about you.
I'm not interested.

You're just an interruption in their mindset.

You can get a lot out of very targeted messaging that boils down your pitch to a hyper simple:

"We do X. Solves Y. Interested?"

But... your ceiling is capped.

Demand creation > capture is how you get out of that cap. "If ___ is true. Would it help if you could ____?"

Pain is the name of the game.

Validate that it exists. Then you can carry the conversation forward.
