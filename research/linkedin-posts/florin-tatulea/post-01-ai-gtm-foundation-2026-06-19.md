Everyone's buying more AI. But in my experience, not enough teams are actively fixing what's underneath it.

We surveyed a group of GTM leaders on what's really blocking AI in their GTM strategy.

The answers had nothing to do with LLMs or models.

Half said their data is too fragmented for AI to make sense of. Half said they can't trust the outputs without constantly double checking them.

The foundation isn't ready, so the AI fills the gaps with noise, and the time savings disappear. Garbage in = garbage out.
