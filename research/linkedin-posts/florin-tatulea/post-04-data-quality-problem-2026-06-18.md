It's 2026 and I STILL get reps emailing me thinking that I work at my previous company which I haven't been at for 6 months. We have all this fancy AI and this is still happening...

That's a fundamental data problem. An LLM model can't fix that problem alone. If you give it bad data, the output is going to be bad. Period.

When we surveyed 50 GTM leaders, it was pretty clear what they wanted: Connected signals, resolved identity, real-time data, outputs they can act on.

They can be that specific because they've already tried the alternatives and watched them fail.
