95% of cold outreach fails.

(in the first 3 seconds)

It's because cold outreach is usually missing one thing…

Context.

Most people jump straight to personalization without laying the groundwork.

Here's the CESPR outbound framework that generates an average of 1-5% reply rate:

Context:
→ What groups are they in?
→ What tools do they use?
→ What content do they engage with?

Enrichment:
→ Company size
→ Funding status
→ Tech stack used

Segmentation:
→ Group similar prospects
→ Match pain points
→ Align messaging

Relevancy:
→ Match offers to segments
→ Address specific challenges
→ Provide segment-specific value

Personalization:
→ Make it about THEM
→ Reference their specific situation
→ Show you've done your homework

Most people start at P and wonder why their outreach fails.

Start with context. Everything else follows.
