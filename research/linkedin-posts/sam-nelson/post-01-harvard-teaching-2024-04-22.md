I always enjoy teaching about outbound prospecting at Harvard Business School.

The other day, I spoke about cold calling and overall outbound strategy and the enthusiasm of the students on the subject was so inspiring!

While some schools turn up their noses at sales or ignore its importance to entrepreneurship, Harvard does not.

And the students definitely don't!

They have an entire entrepreneurial sales program by Mark Roberge and Lou Shipley and one student told me this is one of the most subscribed to classes at Harvard Business School 🤯

Such a cool thing to see…

Huge shout out to Harvard for prioritizing the most important skill in business!
